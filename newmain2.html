Reimagining Urban Spaces

Shade Seekers partners with the Sofia municipality and local citizens to transform underutilized urban areas into welcoming sanctuaries. Our mission is to create a network of beautifully designed, naturally-integrated spaces that offer respite from the bustle of city life.

Through innovative design, community collaboration, and sustainable practices, we're building environments where people can relax, connect, and thrive—one shaded corner at a time.
